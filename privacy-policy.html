Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Information We Collect

Taaza Khabar does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. The app operates without requiring user accounts or personal data.

Data Usage

The app only accesses publicly available news content through news APIs. No user-generated content or personal data is collected or stored.

Internet Connection

The app requires an internet connection to fetch latest news content from external news sources. This data is not stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

We use news APIs to provide content. These services may have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking mechanisms to monitor user behavior.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@taazakhabar.com
